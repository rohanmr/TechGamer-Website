Best games like PUBG Mobile: BGMI, Call of Duty Mobile, Free Fire MAX, and more

Published: 26/03/2022

While the PC version is not as popular, the mobile version was a monumental success in the country with millions of players hooked onto the game ever since its arrival.


However, just after two years of its launch, PUBG Mobile had to face a lot backlash in the country as it was reportedly affecting students especially during examinations. The game was subsequently banned by the Indian government alongside a number of apps due to their Chinese origin and the fact that they posed a threat to national security.


While PUBG Mobile is still banned, there are loads of games that offer similar gameplay including popular battle royale mode.
